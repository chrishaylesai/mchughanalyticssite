## Overview

AI isn't just for big tech companies. With the right approach, small and growing businesses can automate repetitive, high-effort processes using AI agents — and see real results without a massive investment.

I design and build agentic systems that connect your tools, process your data, and handle workflows that would otherwise consume your team's time. The focus is always on outcomes: what can be automated, what should be automated, and how to make it reliable.

## What I can build

- **Automated document processing** — extract, classify, and route information from emails, PDFs, and forms
- **AI-assisted workflows** — agents that draft, summarise, or triage content based on your business logic
- **Tool integrations** — connect LLMs to your existing systems (CRMs, databases, APIs) to create end-to-end automation
- **Monitoring & alerting agents** — intelligent watchers that surface what matters, when it matters

## How it works

Every automation starts with understanding your actual workflow — not a generic template. I'll map the process, identify the highest-value automation opportunities, and build something that fits how your team actually works.

The result is a system you can operate and extend yourself, not a black box that breaks when something changes.

## Technologies

LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source), LangChain, LangGraph, Python, REST APIs, cloud function infrastructure (AWS Lambda, Azure Functions), and whatever tooling already lives in your stack.
