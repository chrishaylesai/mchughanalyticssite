## Overview

Sometimes you don't need someone to build — you need someone to think alongside you. I offer advisory engagements for teams that need senior technical input without the overhead of a full-time CTO or VP of Engineering.

Whether you're choosing a cloud platform, deciding whether to build or buy, planning a migration, or trying to understand why your current setup is struggling, I can help you think through the options and make a confident decision.

## What this looks like

- **Architecture reviews** — an honest assessment of your current setup, with specific recommendations
- **Technology roadmaps** — a pragmatic plan for where you need to get to, and how to get there without burning everything down
- **Vendor and platform selection** — independent analysis of your options, without vendor bias
- **AI readiness assessments** — understand where AI and automation can genuinely help your business, and what you need in place first
- **Fractional CTO input** — ongoing technical leadership for teams that don't yet need a full-time hire

## How I work

I don't produce recommendations I wouldn't stand behind. That means being direct about what I think, including when the answer is "don't change anything yet" or "that vendor isn't right for your needs."

Advisory engagements can be a one-off review, a short sprint, or an ongoing retainer — depending on what you need.

## Who this is for

Founders and leadership teams at SMEs and scale-ups who need experienced technical input at a key decision point: a platform migration, a new product, a growing engineering team, or a shift towards AI and automation.
