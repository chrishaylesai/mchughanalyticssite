## Overview

Whether you're moving off ageing on-premises hardware, rearchitecting a monolith for the cloud, or trying to get your costs and reliability under control, I can help you plan and execute the work.

I've worked extensively across AWS, Azure, and GCP, as well as hybrid and on-premises environments. I don't have a preferred vendor — I'll use the platform that makes the most sense for your situation.

## What I can help with

- **Cloud migrations** — lift-and-shift or rearchitected, with a proper plan rather than an improvised move
- **Infrastructure as code** — clean, version-controlled infrastructure using Terraform, Pulumi, or CDK
- **Cost optimisation** — identify waste, right-size resources, and put guardrails in place
- **Reliability and resilience** — design for failure, implement proper monitoring and alerting
- **Hybrid and on-premises** — on-prem GPU infrastructure, RunPod, hybrid cloud setups
- **CI/CD pipelines** — fast, reliable deployment pipelines that your team can trust

## Platforms covered

AWS (EC2, EKS, Lambda, RDS, S3, and more), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, on-premises infrastructure, hybrid setups.

## What you get

Clear documentation, infrastructure you own and understand, and a setup that a future team member (or yourself six months from now) can actually work with.
